Swiss System pairing software:

1. What will this software do ?
- asks for how many rounds are going to get played
- number of players going to participate
- asks for player names and their chess.com ratings 
- rank them according to their ratings
- for first round pair them fully randomly 
- show standings after first round
- for later rounds pairings will be random + their score 
- show standings after each rounds
- At the end of tournament show ranking according to Buchholz score
